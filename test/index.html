Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lingopie?

Lingopie is the world's only language learning application that uses real TV shows and movies to help you learn a new language. The idea is to make language learning as simple as watching your favorite TV show.

We use real TV shows & movies from the language you want to learn. Each show comes with subtitles in the original language (ex. Spanish TV Show + Spanish Subtitles). Every word, phrase or slang is clickable to give you an instant translation in real-time to help you learn.

After you watch an episode you can easily review all your new vocabulary & grammar rules with our built-in flashcards and word lists.

Lingopie is great for all levels, from beginner to advanced, with great content and language learning tools appropriate for everyone.

What’s included in the Lingopie subscription?

With Lingopie you get access to thousands of hours of great TV shows in the language you want to learn. Every show comes with subtitles in the language you want to learn, as well as English subtitles. You can click on any word or phrase to get an instant translation to help you follow along and learn while you watch.

You also get tons of other great language learning features and tools to help you learn alongside your TV shows.

Where can I watch?

You can watch Lingopie on your phone, computer or tablet. About 50% of our community use Lingopie daily on their phones and it works great!

I'm a beginner. Is Lingopie right for me?

Don't worry! Even if you are a beginner, getting started is easy. You'll find interesting TV shows for any level. Plus you can slow down the video playback speed to make it easier to hear the words. You also get the corresponding English subtitles so that you can easily follow along if you want a more casual binge watching experience.

After just a couple of episodes you'll notice that your comprehension has increased drastically and you can follow along with much of the conversation and dialogue.

How quickly will I learn with Lingopie?

After only 3 or 4 episodes, you'll notice a serious improvement in your listening comprehension and vocabulary. The most important thing is to select a TV series that you'll enjoy.

After a full season of at least 8 episodes your confidence and comprehension will skyrocket. As you watch your brain will be rewired to quickly acquire new words, phrases, slang and grammar from the natural context of the TV shows and movies and you'll see your confidence go through the roof. The more you watch, the more you learn.
